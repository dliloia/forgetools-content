Dave conducted 1,250+ interviews as an Amazon-certified Hiring Bar Raiser. This article shares the most important lessons about evaluating people, building teams, and why most hiring processes fail.

### What Makes a Great Candidate Stand Out
The question is not "did they succeed" but "how did they think"
Ownership: did they take it, or explain why it wasn't theirs?
Judgment: did they see around corners or react to what was obvious?
Impact: did they move things, or observe things?

### Conclusion
The single best thing you can do for your team is hire carefully. 1,250 interviews taught me that most companies don't.
