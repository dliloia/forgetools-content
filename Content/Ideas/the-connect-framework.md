## The Idea
The CONNECT Framework is Dave's structured 30-minute 1:1 format: 10 minutes personal, 10 minutes their work, 10 minutes growth. Nothing on the manager's agenda — that happens the rest of the week. This is 100% for the employee.

## Why it matters
Most 1:1s become status updates or task assignment sessions. That's a waste. The 1:1 is the highest-leverage tool a manager has — the one dedicated space to build trust, learn who your people are, and develop them intentionally. When managers understand that NOTHING in the 1:1 is about their own agenda, the whole relationship shifts.
