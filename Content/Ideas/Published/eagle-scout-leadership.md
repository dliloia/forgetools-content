## The Idea
"Be prepared" isn't just a motto — it's a management philosophy. Eagle Scouts learn to anticipate what they'll need before they need it, serve because it matters (not because it's required), and leave things better than they found them. These three principles are the foundation of everything Dave has built.

## Why it matters
Most managers are reactive. They wait for problems before addressing them, let relationships deteriorate before investing in them, and move on from roles without thinking about what they leave behind. The prepared manager — the one who runs consistent 1:1s, documents everything, develops people before they ask — operates at a different level.
