## The Idea
Dave's leadership philosophy is rooted in a blue collar ethic: you earn respect by doing the work, not by holding the title. Roll up your sleeves, stay humble, show up consistently, and lead by example. This approach — which doesn't come from business school — is often the most effective leadership style in the room.

## Why it matters
There's a kind of leadership that comes from privilege and credential and another that comes from having worked your way up and never forgotten it. The blue collar manager builds teams that are loyal because they've earned that loyalty. The white collar manager often builds teams that perform only under observation.
