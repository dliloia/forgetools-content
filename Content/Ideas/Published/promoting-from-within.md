## The Idea
The managers who build the best teams are the ones who build leaders from within. Identifying promotion-ready talent early, giving them stretch assignments, creating visibility for them, and advocating for them when opportunities arise — this is the highest-leverage development work a manager can do.

## Why it matters
Promoting from within is cheaper, faster, and less risky than external hiring. But it only works if you've been developing people intentionally. The manager who can point to their promoted alumni is the manager who attracts top talent in the first place.
