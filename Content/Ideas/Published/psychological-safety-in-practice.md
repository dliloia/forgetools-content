## The Idea
Psychological safety is one of the most overused phrases in management and one of the least understood in practice. It doesn't mean everyone is comfortable or happy. It means people believe they can speak up, make mistakes, and ask questions without punishment. And it's built — or destroyed — in small moments, many of which happen in 1:1s.

## Why it matters
Google's Project Aristotle found psychological safety was the #1 predictor of high-performing teams. Not talent, not resources, not process. Safety. Teams without it self-censor, hide problems, and under-perform. Teams with it surface issues early, innovate more, and retain people longer.
