## The Idea
AI can transcribe your meetings, extract action items, surface patterns, and draft performance reviews. What it cannot do is build a relationship, read the room, know when to push and when to back off, or earn trust. The human part of management is not automatable — but the administrative weight of it now is.

## Why it matters
Managers who understand where AI adds value and where it doesn't will have a massive advantage over those who either resist it entirely or over-rely on it. The future great manager uses AI to remove friction so they can spend more time on the human work.
