## The Idea
The CONNECT Framework works just as well for 1:1s with your own manager as it does with your direct reports. Most managers forget they're also someone's employee — and most of them manage up poorly.

## Why it matters
Your relationship with your manager determines your ability to protect your team, get resources, advance your career, and do your best work. Treating your 1:1 with your manager the same way you treat your 1:1s downward — prepared, intentional, relationship-focused — is a massive competitive advantage.

## Possible angles
- How to show up prepared for a 1:1 with your manager (not just reactive)
- What your manager actually wants from your 1:1 (hint: not a status update)
- How to use the 1:1 to surface problems before they become fires
- Building trust upward through consistency and follow-through
- Using the 1:1 to advocate for your team
