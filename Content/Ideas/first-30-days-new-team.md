## The Idea
What to do in the first 30 days when you inherit a team — whether you're a new manager or a seasoned one stepping into a new role. Most managers get this wrong by trying to make their mark too fast.

## Why it matters
The first 30 days sets the tone for your entire tenure. Move too fast and you signal that you don't trust the team. Move too slow and you look weak. The right move is listen first, relationship first, change second — and the 1:1 is your primary tool for doing that.

## Possible angles
- The listening tour: how to run your first 1:1 with each team member
- What to look for and what NOT to say in your first round of 1:1s
- How to read team dynamics before you try to change them
- The 30-day questions that reveal everything about your new team
- Why the best managers change nothing in month one
