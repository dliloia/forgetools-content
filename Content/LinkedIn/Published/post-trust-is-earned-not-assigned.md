Your title gives you authority. It does not give you trust.

I've managed hundreds of people and conducted over 1,250 interviews as an Amazon Bar Raiser. Trust is the variable that separates teams that execute from teams that stall. And the place where it's built or destroyed, week by week, is the 1:1.

Most managers don't realize what a canceled 1:1 signals. It's not "I'm busy." It's "you're not a priority." Their team hears it clearly, even when nothing is said out loud.

The math on trust is unfair but simple: deposits are small, withdrawals are large. Boring reliability builds it. One broken promise can wipe out months of it.
