Most managers prep carefully for 1:1s with their team. Then they wing it with their own boss.

That asymmetry costs more than most people realize. Your relationship with your manager determines your ability to protect your team, get resources, and do your best work. And the 1:1 is where that relationship is built or neglected.

Here's the thing: your manager doesn't need a status update. They need to know you see the same problems they see. The managers who figure that out stop showing up to report and start showing up prepared, with something at risk, something they need, something to advocate for. That's a completely different meeting.
