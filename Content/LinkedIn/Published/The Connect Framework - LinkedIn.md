Your 1:1 is not a status update. If you're using it that way, you're wasting your most powerful management tool.

Most 1:1s I've seen go like this: "What are you working on?" "Here's my update." "Ok, see you next week." That's not a 1:1. That's a check-in with a fancy name.

The real version looks different. First ten minutes: them, personally. How are they doing? What's frustrating them? Next ten: their work. Where are they stuck? How can you remove blockers? Last ten: growth. Where do they want to go? What stretch would push them forward?

Notice what's missing: your agenda. Your tasks and priorities can happen any other time. Give them 30 minutes that's actually theirs.
