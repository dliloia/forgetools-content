AI can write your performance reviews. It cannot make them matter.

There's a version of AI-assisted management that actually works, and it looks nothing like what most people assume. It's not about automating the relationship. It's about removing the overhead that keeps you from showing up for it.

The managers I've seen get this right use AI to handle the documentation, the pattern-surfacing, the prep work. Then they spend that recovered time on the conversations that actually change someone's trajectory. The ones where you have to read past what someone is telling you to figure out what's actually wrong.

The ones AI will never be able to have.
