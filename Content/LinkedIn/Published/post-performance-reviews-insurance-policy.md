Most managers dread review season. Here's the actual reason why.

It's not the template. It's not the rating scale. It's that they've been managing by impression all year and now they have to reconstruct six months of work from memory.

The result is reviews full of phrases like "strong contributor" and "works well with others." Sentences that could describe anyone. Reviews that help no one, including you.

The fix isn't a better process at review time. It's 5 bullet points after every 1:1, all year long. That's the whole answer.
